Why Campus Recruitment Training?

Getting a reputable job in a famed organization is a unanimous dream of all Graduates, whether they are from a technical or a non-technical background. Today, as a result of an extremely competitive environment, preparedness to pursue Your dream job is becoming ever more challenging in an urban landscape. Alongside the ever-growing opportunities of the IT industry, there are Other compelling, newer opportunities that have come up. These newer Opportunities provide substantial opportunities for fresh candidates and Experienced laterals, invariably.
Top companies such as Infosys, TCS, Wipro, IBM, Google, CTS etc not only recruit IT man-power but are also constantly looking for Hardware and Networking Profiles, Voice Profiles and HR / Client Co-Ordination Profiles. These are high paying jobs and the growth potential is comparatively high. Hence, their interviews / selection process is rigorous and tough. This is where Flame Technologies, with its accomplished Campus Recruitment Training and Job Placements wing, comes into the picture.
Our time-tested recruitment wing works day and night with aspirants and employers, to provide a win-win maneuver to both ends:

  1. For trainees: Our Campus Recruitment Training Team comprises of globally certified trainers who are proficient in training and getting students interview-ready in no time. The sole aim of our CRT team is to train and place able candidates into reputed organizations, thereby providing you the one dream opportunity and all the possibilities to excel in your professional career.
  2. For Employers: To provide trained, screened and certified candidates to your organization, thereby reducing a notable work-load from your recruitment / placement / HR team.

Our training curriculum includes Written Aptitude Tests, Psychometric Tests, Group Discussions and Personal Interviews along with training in basic requisites like effective resume writing / presentation, interview handling and multi-lingual training for powerful communication skills. Till date, we have Placed over 3000 students into various streams and Multi-national Companies.
